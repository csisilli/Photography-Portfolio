## How to Use This Template For Your Own Portfolio

1. **Fork/Clone** this repository
2. **Delete or replace** all photos in `/client/public/photos/`
3. **Add your own photos** to the same directory
4. **Update** `/client/public/photos.json` with your photo information
5. **Customize** colors, text, and styling in `src/App.css`
6. **Deploy** your portfolio
7. **Optionally** add a link crediting this template (appreciated but not required)
